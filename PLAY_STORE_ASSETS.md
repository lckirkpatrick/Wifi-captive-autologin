# Play Store Assets

This document contains the required assets and information for publishing the app on Google Play Store.

## App Description

### Short Description (80 characters max)
Automatically accept terms on captive portal Wi-Fi networks. No more manual clicking!

### Full Description

**WiFi Captive Auto-Login** - Automatically handle captive portal Wi-Fi networks

Tired of manually clicking "Accept" or "Connect" buttons every time you connect to public Wi-Fi? This app automates the process for you!

**Key Features:**
- ✅ **Automatic Portal Detection** - Detects captive portals when you connect to Wi-Fi
- ✅ **One-Click Setup** - Easy profile configuration for your networks
- ✅ **Smart Matching** - Match networks by exact name, contains, or regex patterns
- ✅ **Profile Templates** - Pre-configured templates for airports, hotels, cafes, and more
- ✅ **Background Operation** - Works automatically in the background
- ✅ **Privacy First** - All data stored locally, no cloud sync required
- ✅ **Encrypted Storage** - Profile data encrypted using Android Keystore

**How It Works:**
1. Add profiles for your Wi-Fi networks (or use templates)
2. Enable the accessibility service (one-time setup)
3. The app automatically detects and accepts portal terms when you connect

**Perfect For:**
- Frequent travelers (airports, hotels)
- Coffee shop visitors
- Public Wi-Fi users
- Anyone tired of manual portal acceptance

**Privacy & Security:**
- No internet connection required for core functionality
- All profiles stored locally on your device
- Encrypted storage for sensitive data
- No tracking or analytics

**Requirements:**
- Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or higher
- Accessibility service must be enabled (required for automation)

**Note:** This app uses Android's Accessibility Service to automatically click portal buttons. The service only interacts with captive portal pages and does not access other apps or sensitive information.
